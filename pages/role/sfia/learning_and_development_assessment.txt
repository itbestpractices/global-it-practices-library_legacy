===== Role: Learning and development assessment (LEDA) =====

==== Description ====
The assessment and evaluation of knowledge and skills acquired by any means whether formal or informal against capability and qualification frameworks such as SFIA..

==== SFIA Level 3 ====
Performs routine assessments of knowl-edge and experience using specified methods and according to specified standards

==== SFIA Level 4 ====
Performs routine and non-routine assessments of knowledge and experience using specified methods and according to specified standards.

==== SFIA Level 5 ====
Administers and ensures the accuracy of knowledge and experience assessments based on specified methods and according to specified standards.

==== SFIA Level 6 ====
Specifies methods and standards for assessing knowledge and experience.
