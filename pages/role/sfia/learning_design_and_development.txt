===== Role: Learning design and development (TMCR) =====

==== Description ====
The design, creation, packaging and mainte-nance of materials and resources for use in learn-ing and development. Typically involves the assimilation of information from existing sources, selection and re-presentation in a form suitable to the intended purpose and audience. Includes design, configuration and testing of learning environments, and use of appropriate current technologies

==== SFIA Level 4 ====
Designs, creates, develops, customises and maintains learning solution content and resources to deliver agreed outcomes. Assists with design, configuration and testing of learning environments, including creation of simulated data, and replication of external systems and interfaces.

==== SFIA Level 5 ====
Specifies the content and structure of learning and development packages. Takes responsibility for design, creation, packaging and maintenance and manages development to deliver agreed outcomes. Where required, designs, configures and tests learning environments, including population of simulated databases, and replication of external systems and interfaces.
